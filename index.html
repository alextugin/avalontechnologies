Sourcing

an OEM is the easiest and most evident source, as their commitment is to have every part in stock.
Such convenience usually comes with much higher price tag to you.


 How many suppliers do you contact to find the needed part?
 How long do you wait for the response?
 Do the received quotes always have the lowest price and the fastest delivery time?
 Finally, how much time do you spend on these tasks?

The Challenge:
Rebuilding

Every manufacturer is striving to reduce the production costs, and competitive market demands higher speeds, greater efficiency, and more automation than ever. Arguably, more than two thirds of all food processing equipment installed is now more than 10 years old. Such machines are still operational, but more and more often they do not meet current requirements for output and efficiency.

 Replacement parts are obsolete or discontinued?
 New parts require complete system overhaul?
 New equipment investment is not budget-friendly?

Solution:
Rebuilding

We have access to suppliers that specialize in discontinued components

If the replacement parts are no longer available, our team may be able to provide upgraded components that would not require a complete overhaul of the machine and its systems.

Our engineering team has extensive experience in designing custom solutions that improve efficiency and productivity of existing machines. These services may include everything from updating electronic controls to redesigning complete modules from the ground up.

Challenge:
Maintenance

Equipment and technologies continue to evolve at the fastest pace ever, and the costs of keeping up with production advancements and maintenance keep rising every year. For small and medium sized companies, the cost of downtime, when a machine breaks down, ordered replacement parts do not arrive on time, maintenance team is stretched between various tasks, is simply unbearable and something that a company cannot afford in todays competitive environment.

Solution:
Maintenance

✓ Inspection

Our team of mechanics will inspect your production lines and perform tests, based on your requirements for production.

✓ Analysis

We will analyze your equipment load, current MRO inventory and stock levels according to your production targets, schedules, and load.

✓ Plan

Based on the inspection and analysis, we will offer a proactive maintenance plan and replacement parts stock levels accordingly.

✓ Savings

Our proactive maintenance plan will feature built-in cost savings. Some of our current customers enjoy 50% and more savings after implementing our program at their production and MRO departments.

Optional proactive maintenance program

Should you wish to outsource your maintenance needs, we can offer our optional proactive maintenance program, as we do know for a fact that finding and retaining qualified staff can be quite difficult. Our team of certified technicians is available to attend to scheduled regular maintenance tasks as well as to respond to emergency situations, when required.
